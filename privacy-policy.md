DVApp Privacy Policy
Last updated: 28 January 2026
Digital Vehicle App (“DVApp”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and stored when you use the app.
1. Information We Collect
DVApp allows users to store MOT-related information including:
Vehicle registration numbers
MOT appointment details (date, time, test centre, lane, booking reference)
Notification preferences
This information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
2. Notifications
DVApp may use push notifications to alert users about MOT appointments.
Notifications are delivered using Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google).
Google may collect limited technical information as described in its privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
3. Data Storage
All MOT appointment data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms.
DVApp does not collect, sell, or share personal data.
4. Third-Party Services
DVApp may interact with third-party services such as:
Google Firebase (notifications)
Northern Ireland DVA website (when opening booking links)
These services operate under their own privacy policies.
5. Data Deletion
Users can delete their data at any time by removing appointments within the app or uninstalling the app.
6. Children’s Privacy
DVApp is not intended for children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
7. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected on this page.
